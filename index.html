How the Indian Stock Market Works

Understand, compare, and explore India's vibrant stock market.

The Indian stock market is a platform for buying and selling shares of publicly listed companies. It operates through two main exchanges: BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange) and NSE (National Stock Exchange). Market timings are from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM (Monday to Friday).

Share prices fluctuate based on demand-supply, economic conditions, and company performance. Market indices like Sensex and Nifty 50 are used to track market trends.
